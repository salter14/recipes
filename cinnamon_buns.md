Bake 12 minutes uncovered, then 15 covered.

### Dough:
- 340.5g milk
- 9g yeast
- 610g flour
- 75g sugar
- 1.5g salt
- 56.5g butter
- 2g vanilla extract
- 2 eggs

### Filling:
- 100g brown sugar 
- 21g cinnamon
- 1.5g cardamom

### Icing:
- 106g cream cheese, softened 
- 71g unsalted butter, softened
- 212g confectioners' sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- bit of milk and salt
- 3-4 teaspoons lemon juice
