Based on this recipe: https://sallysbakingaddiction.com/homemade-cinnamon-swirl-bread/

other variations: https://handletheheat.com/cinnamon-swirl-bread/


Ingredients:
- Water	240g
- Milk	240g
- Yeast	14g
- Sugar	100g
- Butter	112g
- Bread flour	780g
- Salt	12g

Filling:
- 100g brown sugar
- 21g cinnamon
- 1.5g cardamom
- egg white
- melted butter for coating top of loaf

Mix all with stand mixer. Put in greased bowl to rise until double, approx 1-2 hours. Punch down and divide dough in two. Stretch or roll dough into width of loaf pan and 12-18 inches long. Coat with egg white and cover with filling. Roll and place in greased or parchment lined loaf pan (5"x9"). Coat top of loaf with melted butter. Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes on lower rack. Done when internal temp is 195 degrees. Cover with foil if browning too quickly. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes. Remove from pan and allow to cool on cooling rack for at least another 10 minutes.
